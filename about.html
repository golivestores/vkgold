Our Story · Since 2004

The Craft

01
Design

Share your intention with us. Our master artists sketch the deity, motif, or story — interpreted through 700 years of Regong iconographic tradition.

02
Craft

Quzhi and Zhaxijiancuo hand-paint every work over 15+ days — grinding mineral pigments and applying 24-karat gold leaf, one measured stroke at a time.

03
Consecrate

Every finished piece is blessed by lineage monks, signed by the master, and mounted in silk brocade — ready for its insured journey to your home.

Regong Valley · Qinghai Province Masters Quzhi and Zhaxijiancuo at work — a single Thangka takes 15 days or more, stroke by measured stroke, in 24-karat gold.

Direct from the Source

Museum-grade gold craft at honest factory prices — proven by 600,000+ pieces delivered worldwide.

01 · Verified Purity

Every gram weighed. Every piece certified.

Our gold is independently assayed — 99.9% purity (Au999) on blessing stickers, 24K (Au9999) on Thangka gold leaf. A weighing certificate ships with every order, the same standard we supply to 20+ Chinese national banks.

No Middleman
02 · Factory-Direct

Our factory. Our prices.

VKGold owns its 2,000㎡ Shenzhen facility — the same production line that supplies China Gold, Chow Luk Fook, and Laomiao. You pay the factory price, not the retail markup, and we stand behind every gram.
