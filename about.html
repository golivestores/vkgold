About VKGold

Where Sacred Art Meets Modern Craft

Bridging 700 years of Regong Thangka heritage with the discerning collectors of Southeast Asia.

Our Story

Born in Regong, Crafted in Shenzhen, Trusted Across Southeast Asia

Founded by Jinxi Cultural Development, VKGold was born from a singular vision: to bring the sacred art of Regong Thangka painting -- a UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage -- to collectors who value both spiritual depth and exquisite craftsmanship.

Operating from our 2,000-square-metre facility in Shenzhen, we combine ancient Buddhist painting traditions with proprietary PVD gold sputtering technology, delivering museum-grade sacred art and 99.9% pure gold blessing products to the Singapore market and beyond.

Our Process

From Sacred Preparation to Consecration

Step 01
Sacred Preparation

Ritual purification of materials, hand-stretching of cotton canvas on traditional wooden frames, and preliminary sketches guided by iconometric scripture.

Step 02
Gold & Pigment

Application of 24K gold leaf with natural adhesive, grinding of mineral pigments -- malachite, azurite, cinnabar -- mixed with yak-hide glue for lasting vibrancy.

Step 03
Eye-Opening & Consecration

The final sacred act: painting the deity's eyes to invite the divine presence, followed by blessing ceremony and silk brocade mounting.

Our Promise
